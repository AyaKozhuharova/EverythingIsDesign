One day

Client

Date

22nd January 2021

Category

Photography

The project “One day” shows my day in a collection of 10 photos.The goal is to present everyday situations, with natural compositions and turn the routine plot into a gallery of beautiful pictures.The collection is a personal project which aims at showing the basic routine in a different perspective .

The photos are edited with Photoshop CC 2014.I’ve used basic tools from the program, to capture the natural beauty of the pictures and keep the collection together.

Interactive portfolio, created for NOIT 2020/2021.
